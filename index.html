I am Melvin, a research apprentice and undergraduate economics and applied mathematics student at University of California, Berkeley.

Having born in Shanghai and raised Guangzhou as an American citizen abroad, I am proud of my Chinese heritage and my non-traditional background. Prior to coming to Berkeley, I have received an associate's degree in Business and Japanese from Pasadena City College. I am currently the Head of Peer Review at Berkeley Economic Review, and I am honored to be working under Professor Ulrike Malmendier's research team.
